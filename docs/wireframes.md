# Wireframe Plan

## 1. Home

- Large search bar
- Short plain-English mission statement
- "What this does" and "What this does not do" cards
- Example searches
- Demo-mode banner

## 2. Search Results

- Query summary
- Tabs or grouped sections for people, donors, bills, votes
- Small badges for chamber, donor type, and topic

## 3. Politician Profile

- Header with office, party, state, and committee list
- Summary cards for committee money, data freshness, review signals, and why-not-scored counts
- Timeline panel
- Relationship flow panel
- Facts section
- Review signals section
- Why not scored section

## 4. Donor Profile

- Donor organization header
- Industry mapping and confidence label
- Top recipients
- Recent related signals

## 5. Bill Page

- Bill title, congress, chamber, status, and summary
- Topics and referred committees
- Related votes
- Linked signals

## 6. Vote Page

- Vote question and result
- Member vote list
- Related bill and committees
- Linked signals

## 7. Review Signal Guide

- Public eligibility rules
- Signal strength legend
- Explanation that signals are shown only within a single profile

## 8. Methodology

- Factors and weights table
- Severity thresholds
- Limitations and safeguards

## 9. Sources

- Data source cards
- Freshness notes
- Missing-data caveats

## 10. About

- Mission
- Guardrails
- Contribution principles
